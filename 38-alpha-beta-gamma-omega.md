* **Summary:** This work presents a "geometric proof-of-concept" demonstrating that fundamental constants of nature (fine-structure constant Alpha, mass ratio Mu, etc.) are not random parameters, but necessary topological properties of a discrete 3D space lattice. Instead of measuring these values empirically, the paper derives them from minimal geometric axioms.
* **Axioms (The Geometric Basis):** The model relies on two fundamental geometric premises:
  1. **Structure of Space (Kissing Number 12):** A maximally dense lattice in 3D space dictates that every element has 12 neighbors (`K = 12`). This is a mathematical necessity of sphere packing.
  2. **Topology of Matter (Trefoil Knot):** Matter (specifically the proton/neutron) is defined as a "trefoil knot". This is the simplest non-trivial entanglement in 3D space and possesses `3 * 3 = 9` degrees of freedom in phase space.
* **Formulas & Derivations:** The constants emerge from the interplay between the lattice (container) and the knot (content):
  * **Fine-Structure Constant (Alpha^-1):** Defined as the "residual openness" of space.
    * Ideal Capacity: `12^2 = 144` (Square of connectivity).
    * Stabilization Costs: `7` (Geometric loss for stabilizing the knot).
    * **Formula:** `Alpha^-1_geo = 144 - 7 = 137`
  * **Proton-Electron Mass Ratio (Mu):** Calculated as the product of reservoir, object, and coupling.
    * Reservoir (Vacuum Cell): `17` (8 vertices real space + 8 vertices counter-space + 1 center).
    * Object (Trefoil): `9` (Degrees of freedom).
    * Coupling (Lattice): `12` (Neighbors).
    * **Formula:** `Mu = 17 * 9 * 12 = 1836`
  * **The Landé Factor (g):** Arises from the symmetry conflict between a 3-fold object (trefoil) and a 4-fold lattice (cube). The object must rotate twice (720 degrees) to lock in.
    * **Formula:** `g = 720 degrees / 360 degrees = 2`
  * **CMB Temperature (T_CMB):** Interpreted as the frictional heat of the lattice (friction coefficient 1/12).
    * **Result:** `T approx 2.7 K` (Stationary attractor).
* **Results:** The model provides exact geometric base values, which are adjusted to measurable reality by "context operators" (density, thermics, scale):
  * `Alpha^-1 approx 137.036` (via thermal slip).
  * Proton radius `approx 0.8759 fm` (via scale operator `1 + 1/24`).
  * Hubble constant `H_0 approx 73.02 km/s/Mpc` (via density operator `1 + 1/12`).
* **Philosophical Context:**
  * **Geometry instead of Parameterization:** The work argues that the universe has no free parameters ("God did not twiddle the dials"), but executes a single geometric blueprint.
  * **Context Physics:** Anomalies like the Hubble tension or the proton radius puzzle are interpreted as misunderstandings where one has forgotten to account for the geometric context (lattice density, measurement scale).
  * **Monocausality:** All phenomena (gravity, electromagnetism, mass) are traced back to a single cause: The conflict of a 3-axis vortex within a 4-axis lattice.

Anstatt diese Werte empirisch zu messen, leitet das Paper sie aus minimalen geometrischen Axiomen ab.
* **Axiome (Die geometrische Basis):** Das Modell stützt sich auf zwei fundamentale geometrische Prämissen:
  1. **Struktur des Raumes (Kusszahl 12):** Ein maximal dichtes Gitter im 3D-Raum erzwingt, dass jedes Element 12 Nachbarn hat (`K = 12`). Dies ist eine mathematische Notwendigkeit der Kugelpackung.

* **Formeln & Herleitungen:** Die Konstanten ergeben sich aus dem Zusammenspiel zwischen dem Gitter (Container) und dem Knoten (Inhalt):

The event horizon is interpreted optically as an "Obsidian Interface"—a transducer that absorbs 3D light and converts it into 4D signals, which appears to us as absolute colorlessness (blackness).
* **Axioms:**
  1. **Saturation Limit:** The complexity of 3D matter is limited. When the atomic matrix (9 channels) is saturated (`N = 81`), further pressure forces a dimensional jump.
  2. **Ontological Breach:** The event horizon is not a mere coordinate in space, but the hard phase boundary to an ontologically orthogonal dimension (4D). There is no shared vacuum continuum, therefore no entanglement across the boundary.
* **Formulas:**
  * **The 3D Saturation:** `Limit_3D = 9^2 = 81` (Basis for heavy elements).
  * **The Capacity Gradient:**
    `F_3D = 3^3 = 27` (Cubic degrees of freedom).
    `F_4D = 4^4 = 256` (Hypercubic degrees of freedom).
    (The gradient `27 -> 256` generates the massive entropic suction of gravity).
  * **The Projection Invariant:** `N_Horizon = 81^2` (The maximum projection of the 4D core onto the 3D surface).
* **Results:**
  * **Refutation of Hawking Radiation:** Since 3D vacuum fluctuations cannot couple to the 4D core (lack of a common basis), evaporation is physically impossible. Black holes are stable.
  * **Obsidian Analogy:** The black hole is not "black" (pigment), but "colorless" (absence). Light is not trapped, but transduced into an unreadable signal form (4D).
  * **Gravitational Waves:** Strong GW signals are the specific signature of topological phase transitions (3D to 4D), during which the vacuum is orthogonally excited.
* **Philosophical Context:** The paper reinterprets the "darkness" in the universe. Black holes are not holes in space, but **crystals** in a higher dimension ("luxury states" of matter). They are the proof that 3D space is only a subset of reality. Jets are the energetic "valves" through which these high-pressure systems interact with the vacuum.

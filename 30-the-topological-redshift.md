* **Summary:** This paper executes a radical break with standard cosmology by interpreting cosmic redshift not kinematically (as escape velocity/expansion), but optically (as transmission impedance). The universe is modeled as a static, structured waveguide (NFD lattice). Light continuously loses energy on its path through this medium ("Tired Light" reloaded, but without scattering), which is measured as redshift. The central breakthrough of the work is the quantitative explanation of the Hubble tension: The discrepancy between the early and late universe (67 vs. 73 km/s/Mpc) is not a measurement error, but results from the different optical density of the vacuum in voids (empty) and filaments (matter-saturated).
* **Axioms:**
  1. **Vacuum as a Waveguide:** Space is not an empty nothingness, but a physical medium with a defined impedance (resistance) for light propagation.
  2. **Topological Slip:** In regions of high matter density (filaments), light additionally couples to the geometric structure of the densest packing (kissing number 12), which increases the effective resistance.
* **Formulas:**
  * **The Path Integral of Impedance:** Redshift `z` is the measure of cumulative attenuation along the path `S`.
    `z = exp( Integral( alpha(r) dl ) ) - 1`
  * **The Hubble Bifurcation:** The local Hubble constant (`H_local`) results from the base value (`H_CMB`) multiplied by the geometric structure factor.
    `H_local = H_CMB * (1 + 1/12)`
  * **The Numerical Solution:**
    `73.02 = 67.4 * (1 + 0.0833...)`
* **Results:**
  * **Resolution of the Hubble Tension:** The model predicts the locally measured SH0ES value (73.04 km/s/Mpc) from the Planck data (67.4 km/s/Mpc) with a precision of 99.97%, without free parameters.
  * **Time Dilation as Dispersion:** The observed time dilation in supernovae is not explained as relativistic time dilation, but as signal dispersion (analogous to pulses in optical fibers) due to lattice interaction.
* **Philosophical Context:** The work elevates the Hubble tension from the status of an annoying anomaly to the empirical proof of the granular structure of space. If space has a structure (12 neighbors), it *must* attenuate light. Expansion is an optical illusion; the universe is static and infinitely old.

* **Zusammenfassung:**
